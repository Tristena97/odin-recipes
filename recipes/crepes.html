Powdered Sugar Crepes

Description:

From France to your kitchen! This recipe will be easy for even those who shy away from the kitchen!

Ingredients:

  • Four eggs
  • 1/3 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of all purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 2 tbsp melted sugar
  • Powdered sugar

How to Cook:

  1. Mix all the above ingredients until no lumps are left
  2. Butter a nonstick pan and place over medium heat
  3. Pour batter onto the pan until a thin layer has been created. Tip: Tilting the pan will spread the batter out to ensure a thin and even crepe
  4. Cook for 30 seconds to 1 minute; only one side is typically cooked
  5. Remove the crepe from the pan; add desired amount of powdered sugar
  6. Enjoy!

Chicken Cutlets

Description:

This recipe will be going over how to make chicken cutlets. It will take approximently 35 minutes to cook and has 4 to 6 servings.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breast(roughly 2 pounds)
  • 1 cup flour
  • Salt/ground black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons granulated garlic
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Pecorino-Romano
  • 1 stick unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Lemon wedges optional

Instructions:

  1. Clean and cut chicken breast into roughly 8 equal peieces. Pound chicken starting from the center working outwards until the chicken is 1/4th inch thick.
  2. Set up the breading station. mix 1 tablespoon of salt, 1 teaspoon granulated garlic, 1 teaspoon onion powder, 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper, and 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes into a shallow dish
  3. Beat the eggs with 1 tablespoon water in a seperate shallow dish
  4. Mix the panko, Pecorino-Romano, 1 tablespoon salt, the remaining garlic and onion powder. 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper and the remaining red pepper flakes. This should be in a seeprate third shallow dish.
  5. Coat each cutlet first in the flour mix, followed by the egg mixture and lastly the breadcrumb mixture.
  6. Grab your large skillet and heat 2 tablespoons of butter and 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium-low heat.
  7. Add 2 pieces at a time and cook until the breading is golden in color. roughly 3-5 minutes per side. Then their ready to serve!
